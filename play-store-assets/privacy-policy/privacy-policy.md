# TuTu Browser Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** March 18, 2026

## Introduction

TuTu Browser ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile browser application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Bookmarks**: We store your bookmarked websites locally on your device
- **Browsing History**: We save your last visited URL locally for session restore functionality
- **Settings**: We store your app preferences (theme, HTTPS preference, etc.) locally

### Information We Do NOT Collect
- **Browsing Data**: We do not collect or transmit your browsing history, visited websites, or search queries
- **Personal Information**: We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any personally identifiable information
- **Location Data**: We do not access or collect your location
- **Device Information**: We do not collect device identifiers, IP addresses, or usage analytics

## How We Use Your Information

All data is stored **locally on your device** and is used solely for:
- Providing bookmark functionality
- Restoring your previous browsing session (if enabled)
- Remembering your app settings and preferences

## Data Storage and Security

- All data is stored locally using Android's secure DataStore
- No data is transmitted to external servers
- No third-party analytics or tracking services are used
- Your browsing activity remains private and on your device

## Third-Party Services

TuTu Browser uses the following third-party services:
- **Websites you visit**: When you browse websites, those sites may collect data according to their own privacy policies
- **Google Play Services**: Required for app distribution and updates

## Children's Privacy

TuTu Browser is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
