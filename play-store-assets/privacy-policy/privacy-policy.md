# Nova Browser (TuTu) Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** April 24, 2026

## Introduction

Nova Browser, also known as TuTu Browser ("we," "our," or "us"), is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile browser application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Bookmarks**: We store your bookmarked websites locally on your device
- **Browsing History**: We save your visited URLs, page titles, timestamps, and visit counts locally for history functionality and session restore
- **Settings**: We store your app preferences (theme, HTTPS, fullscreen, ad blocking, desktop mode, etc.) locally
- **Custom Search Engines**: We store user-added search engines (name and query URL) locally
- **User Scripts**: We store Tampermonkey-style user scripts you add or create locally
- **Site Settings**: We store per-domain settings (JavaScript, ad block, desktop mode, cookies, zoom, user-agent) locally
- **Download Records**: We store download metadata (URL, filename, MIME type, file path, progress, status) locally

### Information We Do NOT Collect
- **Browsing Data**: We do not transmit your browsing history, visited websites, or search queries to any server
- **Personal Information**: We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any personally identifiable information
- **Location Data**: We do not access or collect your location
- **Device Information**: We do not collect device identifiers, IP addresses, or usage analytics
- **Network Logs**: Network request logs are stored locally only and are not transmitted externally

## How We Use Your Information

All data is stored **locally on your device** and is used solely for:
- Providing bookmark, history, and download management functionality
- Restoring your previous browsing session (if enabled)
- Remembering your app settings, custom search engines, and user scripts
- Applying per-site settings and user scripts to websites you visit
- Network request logging for debugging (stored locally only)

## Data Storage and Security

- App settings are stored locally using Android's secure DataStore
- Bookmarks, history, downloads, site settings, and user scripts are stored locally using Room database
- No data is transmitted to external servers operated by us
- No third-party analytics, tracking, or telemetry services are used
- Your browsing activity, network logs, and personal data remain private and on your device

## Import and Export

The app provides **Import/Export** functionality that allows you to:
- Export all your data (settings, bookmarks, history, site settings, user scripts, custom search engines) to a JSON file on your device storage
- Import data from a previously exported JSON file

**Important:** Exported backup files are stored on your device's local storage. You are responsible for securing these files. We do not have access to exported backups.

## User Scripts

User scripts you install may run on websites you visit. These scripts:
- Are executed within the context of the webpage
- May access and modify page content, cookies, and local storage
- May make network requests to third-party servers

**We do not review, control, or take responsibility for user scripts.** Please only install scripts from trusted sources.

## Ad Blocking

The app includes a built-in ad blocker that:
- Blocks network requests to known ad and tracker domains using a local block list
- Intercepts network requests locally on your device
- Does not send your browsing data to any external ad-blocking service

## Third-Party Services

Nova Browser uses the following third-party services:
- **Websites you visit**: When you browse websites, those sites may collect data according to their own privacy policies
- **Google Play Services**: Required for app distribution and updates via Google Play Store

## Children's Privacy

Nova Browser is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
